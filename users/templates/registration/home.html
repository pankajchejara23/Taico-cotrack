{% blocktrans %} CoTrack is a Learning Analytics tool which allows monitoring of collaborative learning activity in classrooms. The tool has been developed over the period of 5 years by the researchers at the Center of Education Technology, Tallinn University, Estonia.

The tool has got international recognition, winning " Best Education Technology Demonstration Award " at the presigious confernce of Learning Analytics & Knowledge in the year of 2023 in the USA .

CoTrack can be used for to conduct group activities in classroom using an environment similar to Google Doc, to monitor group dynamics & participation level of each individual or to collect data (e.g., audio, video, logs)
for research purposes. {% endblocktrans%}
